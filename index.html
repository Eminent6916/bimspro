PRODUCT OBJECTIVE

BIMS will capture and analyse key farm input data to improve safety, productivity & profitability by up to 50%. By using simple but effective user interface, small holder farmers can make immediate data driven decision to reduce mortality and improve the average culling weight. Consequently, improving the livelihood of small-scale poultry broiler farmers and eventually reducing poverty

1. Maximise Profit

Assist small holder broiler farmers and other players in the broiler chicken value chain to maximise and optimise operations.

2. Operational Capacity

BIMS has the capacity to monitor and evaluate the small holder broiler farms with combined stocking capacity of 10,000,000 birds per production cycle.

3. Multi sided SaaS Agfintech

BIMS has a multi sided SaaS Agfintech platform that will improve the average weight per bird and reduce the average mortality.
